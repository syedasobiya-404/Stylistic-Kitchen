Creating the perfect outdoor summer oasis at home

What better way to enjoy the great outdoors over the summer than by turning your backyard into a summertime outdoor oasis? There are many methods to make your outside space into a calming and pleasant hideaway, whether you have a big garden or a small balcony.

Summers are incomplete without the bright and warm yellow sun rays, golden hours and flowers dancing to the slight warm breeze. Here is everything you need if you love the colour yellow for your summers!

Add colours to your summer snacking time

When the heat strikes and you enjoy a plate full of nachos and salsa, or hummus and pita with veggies. This amber love chip and dip plate is here to serve it all without any hassle. You can refill the dip bowl as needed. This product has beige and yellow tones with minor detailing over ceramics, making it a perfect example of form and function. You can indeed include this as an appetiser along with some refreshing drinks.

Making you beat the heat with butter up

There is always room for dessert on the table. Although last but not least, it can be a showstopper at your dinner and brunch parties. A mango pudding or a mango parfait would be a perfect way to add more yellow to the table. You can stack your dessert ramekins on a wisteria yellow cake stand to elevate your tablescapes and make the most of summer.

Yellow is known to emit positivity and warmth. It is often paired with sunshine, which creates the perfect summer oasis that you love to create with your family, friends, or on a brunch date.
