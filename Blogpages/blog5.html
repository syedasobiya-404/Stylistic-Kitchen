The importance of hydration for the summer

The sun is shining brightly, and the temperature is rising. It’s a beautiful time of the year, but it can also be exhausting if we don’t take care of ourselves. The secret to staying fresh and energetic throughout the summer is to keep ourselves hydrated. At ellementry, we believe that hydration should be a delightful experience, and that’s why we’ve curated a range of drinkware products that not only keep your drinks cool but also add a refreshing touch to your summer table. So, let’s raise a glass to a refreshing and revitalising summer season!

saan ceramic jug and saan ceramic tumbler

One of the best ways to stay hydrated during the summer is to drink plenty of fluids throughout the day and to make sure that you always have access to these fluids and have enough of them to keep you hydrated, you can use the saan ceramic jug.

This jug is an ideal choice to serve your favourite beverages like water, cocktails, or fresh juice during the hot summer days. It features charming hand-painted chequered-weave designs that will certainly warm the hearts of your loved ones. The blue textured details add a touch of depth and intricacy. Pair it with saan ceramic tumblers from the same collection to share your favourite beverages with everyone at the dinner table.

terracotta water tumbler

The ancient matka, a symbol of Indian culture and tradition, has now undergone a stunning transformation into a modern avatar. The terracotta water tumbler is a manifestation of this timeless piece, crafted with a contemporary twist, so you can carry it outside your kitchen with ease and keep your beverages cool and fresh.

From the charming hand-painted Saan jug and glass to the eco-friendly terracotta water tumbler, there’s something to quench everyone’s thirst.
