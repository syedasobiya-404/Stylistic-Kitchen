SHIPPING POLICY

Order Processing:

a. Processing Time: We strive to process and prepare orders for shipment as quickly as possible. Orders are typically processed within 1-3 business days, excluding weekends and holidays. However, during peak seasons or promotional periods, processing times may be slightly longer.

b. Order Verification: To prevent fraud and ensure the security of our customers, we may perform order verification procedures. This may include contacting you to confirm your order details or requesting additional information. Failure to provide the required information within a reasonable time may result in order cancellation.

Shipping Methods and Delivery:

a. Shipping Carriers: We work with reputable shipping carriers to deliver your orders. The available carriers and shipping methods will be displayed during the checkout process. The selection of carriers may vary based on your location.

b. Shipping Destinations: We currently offer shipping to addresses within the specified regions or countries mentioned on our website. If your location is not listed, we regret that we cannot fulfill your order at this time.

c. Shipping Costs: Shipping costs are calculated based on the weight, dimensions, and destination of your order. The applicable shipping charges will be displayed during the checkout process before you finalize your purchase.

d. Estimated Delivery Time: The estimated delivery time depends on the shipping method selected and the destination. Please note that the estimated delivery time is provided by the shipping carrier and may vary due to factors beyond our control, such as customs delays or unforeseen circumstances.

Damaged or Lost Shipments:

a. Packaging: We take utmost care in packaging and securing your order to ensure its safe transit. If you receive a visibly damaged package, please refuse the delivery and contact us immediately. If you accept a damaged package, please document the damages and notify us within 48 hours of receipt.

b. Lost Shipments: In the rare event that your shipment is lost in transit, please contact us, and we will work with the shipping carrier to resolve the issue. We may initiate an investigation with the carrier to locate your package or arrange a replacement or refund, depending on the outcome of the investigation.

Order Modifications and Cancellations:

a. Modifications: Once an order is placed, it may not be possible to modify the order details, including shipping address, items, or quantities. If you need to make any changes, please contact us as soon as possible, and we will do our best to accommodate your request.
